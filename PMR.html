Multi-Functional Robotic Workcell

I lead development on a Epson SCARA robot workcell called a PMR. The work on the PMR work cell resulted in multiple processes being automated including:

  • Gemstone Weighing
  • Vision Guided Pick & Place
  • Precision Glue Dispense & Part Mounting

I procured 10 of these systems by working with contract manufacturers to build to print multiple workcells during 3 seperate build cycles.

One of the biggest concerns from management was to keep capitol investments low, so 1 robot had to be able to accomplish many tasks. The the robot is able to idenify product with a camera, pick & place items, dispense glue accuratley, and weigh product.
